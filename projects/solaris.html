Overview

Nestled within an influential vicinity, Solaris emerges as a masterpiece of contemporary architecture, seamlessly integrating with the city's dynamic skyline. Its striking silhouette and modern design evoke a sense of awe, offering an enchanting living space for discerning individuals who appreciate exquisite craftsmanship and natural beauty.

Featuring 12 floors of semi-private corridors, Solaris ensures exclusivity and tranquility, elevating the residential experience. Each residence boasts expansive sundecks, providing panoramic views of the surroundings and fostering a deep connection with nature through open green spaces.

With an array of over 50 amenities, Solaris caters to every lifestyle need, from leisure and recreation to sustainable living practices. From the majestic entrance to its innovative solar-powered infrastructure, Solaris sets a new standard of aspirational luxury living.

Privacy is paramount, with heightened entrances ensuring optimal ventilation and seclusion. Solaris isn't just a dwelling; it's a sanctuary where life is enriched, promising a lifestyle of exquisite sophistication and comfort.

Specifications

Framed Structure

R.C.C framed structure with earth quake resistance design.

Super Structure

Cement brick with 9 inchs thick external and 4.5 inches internal.

Doors and windows

Main Door : Main door of size 4’x7′ or 4 ‘x8’, indian teak wood frame and shutters with architrave All bedroom doors size 3 ‘3 “x7’0″ or 3’3″x8’0”, frame with indian teak and flushdoor shutters.
Windows: All windows, french windows, and french doors will be in upvc of aparna, koemmerling, fenesta, prominance. Veka, ncl wintech etc.

Bathrooms

Cp fittings – grohe, kohler, moen, hindware, delta, jaguar.
Sanitary fittings: parryware, kohler, hindware, cera.

Kitchen

Provision for water purifier, chimney, hub.
Separate water connection for raw water and drinking water near sink.
Glazed ceramic tile dado upto 2′ height above kitchen platform.

Painting

External wall: texture or putty finish with two coats of exterior emulsion paint of Asian make.
All Internal walls: All walls and ceilings with double coat putty and finished with two coats of tractor emulsion of royale emulsion paint of Asian make.

Dado and Cladding

Anti skid ceramic flooring of size 1 x1′ tiles.
Tile dado upto lintel level of size 1 x1′ tiles.

Flooring

Flat interior flooring with double charged vitrified tiles of size 2×4 in living area and 2’x2.
In all other areas of kajaria or johnson or bandhan or navin or equivalent.
All external staircases and coridors with granite flooring.
All service staircases and services lift lobbies with kota stone flooring.
All main entrance lobbies with 4’x4′ double charged vitrified tiles of same make as no. 1.

Lifts

12 passengers, otis, schindler, kone, johnson.

Electrical

Concealed copper wire of polycab, finolex, rr cable or equivalent.
Switches: legrand, schneider, hafele, salzer, anchor or equivalent.

Power Back-up

100% power backup for common amenities and lights and fans for all flats.

Security

All common areas will be provided with cc cams, intercom at main entrance security room and solar fencing along the boundary wall.
