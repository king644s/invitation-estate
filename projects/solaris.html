Overview

Nestled within an influential vicinity, Solaris emerges as a masterpiece of contemporary architecture, seamlessly integrating with the city's dynamic skyline. Its striking silhouette and modern design evoke a sense of awe, offering an enchanting living space for discerning individuals who appreciate exquisite craftsmanship and natural beauty.

Featuring 12 floors of semi-private corridors, Solaris ensures exclusivity and tranquility, elevating the residential experience. Each residence boasts expansive sundecks, providing panoramic views of the surroundings and fostering a deep connection with nature through open green spaces.

With an array of over 50 amenities, Solaris caters to every lifestyle need, from leisure and recreation to sustainable living practices. From the majestic entrance to its innovative solar-powered infrastructure, Solaris sets a new standard of aspirational luxury living.

Privacy is paramount, with heightened entrances ensuring optimal ventilation and seclusion. Solaris isn't just a dwelling; it's a sanctuary where life is enriched, promising a lifestyle of exquisite sophistication and comfort.

Project Highlights

  1. Architectural Features
    Solaris boasts sleek, modern design with floor-to-ceiling windows and contemporary finishes, defining luxury living
  2. Landscaping
    Impeccably manicured gardens and serene open spaces create a tranquil oasis amidst the urban landscape.
  3. Club Amenities
    Residents enjoy access to a state-of-the-art clubhouse, offering exclusive recreational and social spaces for relaxation and entertainment.
  4. Sports and Games Facilities
    From a fully equipped fitness center to tennis courts and gaming zones, Solaris caters to active lifestyles with diverse sports and leisure facilities.
  5. Responsible Living
    With sustainable practices at its core, Solaris incorporates solar power, recycling initiatives and eco-friendly materials for environmentally conscious living.
  6. Security Arrangements
    Residents' safety is paramount, ensured through 24/7 surveillance, access control systems and trained security personnel.
